Understanding discord channels   

Anti Cheat

Here is where you will understand how anti cheat flags work 

Understanding How It Works

When an anti cheat notification comes in, they are sent into the discord channel named #anti-cheat. From there, you need to evaluate whether the player is cheating or not..

Understanding Basic Different Anti Cheat Flags

"Enabled a form of GODMODE" is a flag that the anti cheat never gets wrong.

"attempt to RESET the character of" is when a hacker tries to reset a player. This isalso a flag that the anti cheat never gets it wrong

"Created (prop) object in" is whenever a hacker tries to spawn in a prop/object. The anti cheat never gets it wrong.

"Loaded a MOD MENU with INSTRUCTIONAL BUTTONS" is whenever a hacker opens a mode menu, sometimes the menu doesn't appear on the screen since it is seperate from the game. The anti-cheat gets this right most of the time.

Banning players

ALL types of cheating is punishable with a perm ban.

You can ban players by accessing TxAdmin https://txa.megasgames.com
