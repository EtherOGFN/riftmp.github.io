How to install and use EasyInstaller

How do I download Fortnite 9.41?

Downloading EasyInstaller

1) Download EasyInstaller from riftmp.com

2) When it's finished downloading, open the exe file, it should look something like this when you open it

You will see a list of Fortnite Versions, the version we are trying to download is 9.41.
Since we are downloading 9.41, we will want to enter the number "21" and then press Enter.

Then, EasyInstaller will ask you to input a Game Folder Location.
You will need to tell it where you want 9.41 to be downloaded on your Computer.

For Example:
D:\!RIFT\9.41
When you have selected the folder, press Enter

When Fortnite 9.41 finishes downloading, continue on to the next Article on downloading the Launcher
